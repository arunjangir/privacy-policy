Your Rights

Data Deletion

Request permanent removal of your personal data from Vinkasa

You have the right to be forgotten. Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and our commitment to your privacy, you may request the deletion of your personal data from Vinkasa at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

01 What Gets Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your full name and WhatsApp phone number
  • Your work preferences and preferred cities
  • Your resume file stored on our servers (Cloudflare R2)
  • Your conversation session history
  • Your profile visibility settings
  • All data shared with your profile entry
⚠️ Note on Payment Records: Transaction IDs and payment confirmation records (not card/UPI details) may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Indian financial and tax laws (GST Act, Income Tax Act). These records do not contain your name or contact details beyond what was submitted to PhonePe.

02 How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion through any of the following methods:

1
