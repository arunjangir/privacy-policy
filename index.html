About Vinkasa

Vinkasa is a proprietorship enterprise owned by Arun Jangir and registered under the Udyam Registration system of the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India. The enterprise operates from Alwar, Rajasthan, India.

Our Business Activities

  • Dairy Manufacturing: Manufacture of cream, butter, cheese, curd, ghee, khoya and other dairy products.
  • Employment Placement Services: Collection and processing of candidate resumes via digital platforms and facilitating job opportunities by forwarding profiles to recruitment agencies and employers.

Privacy & Data Protection

We respect individual privacy and are committed to protecting personal information. Candidate data collected through our services is used strictly for employment-related purposes and may be shared with recruitment partners for job placement.
